Hangman Rules

How to Play

  • A random word is chosen from a specific category
  • Click on letters to guess the word
  • Correct guesses reveal the letter in the word
  • Wrong guesses add a part to the hangman drawing

Winning & Losing

  • Win by guessing all letters in the word before the hangman is complete
  • Lose if the hangman drawing is completed (6 wrong guesses)

Scoring

  • Earn points for each correct word guessed
  • Bonus points for guessing with fewer wrong attempts
  • Use hints if you get stuck (costs points)
